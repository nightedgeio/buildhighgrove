Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide when you call, text, email, mail, or submit a form: name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Property information you share with us about land or property you own or represent (address, condition, asking price).
  • Correspondence records — copies of the messages you exchange with us.

We do not collect payment card data on this website, and this website sets no advertising or tracking cookies.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and communicate about a property or transaction.
  • To evaluate properties and prepare offers you have asked us to consider.
  • To meet legal, tax, and title/escrow obligations in a real-estate transaction.

What we never do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your mobile number or SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes.
  • We do not send marketing text messages to anyone who has not opted in.

We share information only with service providers who help us operate (e.g., title companies, attorneys, transaction platforms) and only as needed for a transaction you are part of, or when required by law.

SMS / text messaging terms

If you opt in to text messaging with Highgrove — by texting us first, providing your number and asking us to text you, or checking an opt-in box — the following applies:

  • Purpose: conversational messages about your property, our offer, scheduling, and transaction updates. Message frequency varies with your conversation.
  • Opt out any time: reply STOP to end messages. Reply HELP for help or email deals@buildhighgrove.com.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third party for marketing.

Data retention & your rights

We keep correspondence and transaction records as long as needed for business and legal purposes. You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by emailing deals@buildhighgrove.com. We honor applicable rights under Texas and other state privacy laws.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page with an updated date.
